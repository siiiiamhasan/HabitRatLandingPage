About HabitRat

Building better habits, one day at a time

Our Mission

HabitRat was created with a simple mission: to help people build and maintain positive habits through an intuitive, beautiful, and motivating interface. We believe that small, consistent actions lead to remarkable transformations in life.

What We Do

HabitRat is a habit tracking application designed to make building new habits effortless and enjoyable. Whether you want to drink more water, read daily, exercise regularly, or develop any positive routine, HabitRat provides the tools and motivation you need to succeed.

Key Features

  • Daily Tracking: Simple interface to check off habits each day and build momentum
  • Analytics: Beautiful charts and insights to understand your progress over time
  • Streak System: Powerful streak tracking to keep you motivated and consistent
  • Cloud Sync: Access your habits across all your devices seamlessly
  • Smart Reminders: Custom notifications to help you stay on track
  • Categories: Organize your habits with custom tags and categories

Available Now

HabitRat is currently available on Android. We're continuously working to improve the app and add new features based on user feedback.

Our Philosophy

We believe in simplicity, consistency, and progress over perfection. HabitRat is designed to be minimal yet powerful, giving you just what you need to track your habits without overwhelming you with unnecessary complexity. Every feature is thoughtfully designed to support your journey toward lasting change.
