Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to HabitRat. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: If you sign in using Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: Device information, OS version, and app interaction data to improve your experience.
  • App Data: Your habits, logs, diamonds, and streaks are securely stored in our cloud databases to sync across your devices.

3. Use of Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the HabitRat Service.
  • Sync your progress securely across devices.
  • Process subscriptions securely.
  • Serve personalized ads (for non-premium users).
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

4. Third-Party Services

HabitRat utilizes trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services & Firebase: For authentication, secure database hosting, and analytics.
  • RevenueCat: For securely managing HabitRat Pro subscriptions.
  • AdMob: For displaying advertisements (applicable to free users).

5. Data Retention

Your personal data and habit progress are retained as long as your account is active. We maintain your data to ensure seamless cross-device synchronization and account recovery.

6. HabitRat Account & Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your account and associated data from HabitRat, please follow these steps:

  • 1. Open the HabitRat app.
  • 2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  • 3. Confirm your request to delete your account.
  • 4. All personal data including your Google account info, app settings, and progress data will be permanently deleted.
  • 5. Any anonymous or aggregated data used for analytics may be retained.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols (Firebase Security Rules) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

HabitRat does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or for further questions regarding data deletion, please contact us at habitrat.support@gmail.com.
